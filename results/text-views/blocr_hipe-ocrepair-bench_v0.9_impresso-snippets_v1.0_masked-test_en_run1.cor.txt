===== WMTG-1895-02-12-a-p0002_par109 =====
POLICE MATRONS. —Mr. Asquith has sent a
letter to Miss Balgarnie stating that, as a temporary
measure, such steps have been taken as will
ensure that all females conveyed to a police station
and detained in consequence of the court not sitting shall be seen by a matron. The Home
Secretary hopes that before long satisfactory arrangements of a permanent character will be
devised in reference to this matter.
